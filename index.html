Mechatronics Engineering

Introduction

Mechatronics engineering or Mechatronics is the a branch of engineering which combines mechanical, electrical, electronic and software engineering to create computer-controlled machines.

The field of mechatronics was originally intended to be nothing more than a combination of mechanics and electronics, hence the name, which is a portmanteau of mechanics and electronics; however, as technical systems became more complicated, the definition was broadened to cover more technical domains.
